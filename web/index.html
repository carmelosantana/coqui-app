Your AI agent, in your browser

A privacy-first chat client for the Coqui AI agent. Runs entirely in your browser via WebAssembly. No server-side code, your data stays on your device.

Private by design
All data stored locally in your browser. No cookies, no analytics, no server-side state.
WASM-powered
Full SQLite database and native-speed execution via WebAssembly. Install as a PWA for an app-like experience.
Connect anywhere
Works with local or remote Coqui API servers. You control where your agent runs.
Cross-platform
Also available as a native app for macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android.
